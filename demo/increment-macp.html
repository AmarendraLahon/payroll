Increment, Fixation & MACP §07

Increment = column move (cell+1). Promotion / MACP = row jump + re-fixation. Each emits a Service Event and re-derives Basic.

Increment & MACP rules (7th CPC)

Annual Increment

  • Two increment dates: 1 January and 1 July.
  • Minimum 6 months service in the level on the increment date.
  • Normal increment = next cell (≈3%, baked into the matrix).
  • EOL / dies-non can defer the next increment date.

MACP (non-teaching)

  • Financial upgradation at 10, 20, 30 years of service.
  • Moves to the next Pay Level (same duties, higher pay).
  • Max 3 upgradations — promotions count against the quota.

Teaching staff progress by CAS (UGC Regulations 2018), not MACP — separate eligibility, same fixation mechanics.
